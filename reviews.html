iPhone 7 -

This is everything you already know and love about Apple's smartphones, and more. The new and improved 12MP camera delivers flawless photos. Water-resistance means you don't have to worry about a splash of rain. And stereo speakers invite you to enjoy music like never before.

Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge -

With the first Dual Pixel 12MP camera, a curved Quad HD screen and a premium metal and glass design, the Samsung Galaxy S7 edge is the best, most cutting-edge Galaxy phone ever.

HTC 10 -

The HTC 10 is the most advanced smartphone from the Taiwanese company to date, with an amazing new camera and brand new look and feel to the software.

LG G5 -

The LG G5 is the best LG smartphone to date. With an all-metal design, dual camera and a slide-out battery, it's one of the most exciting flagships of the year.
